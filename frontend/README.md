## 🌟 Felicity – Special Needs Support Platform

**Felicity** is a web-based platform designed to help parents of children with special needs easily understand conditions and find the right support services such as doctors, therapists, schools, training centers, and support organizations.

---

## 🎯 Problem Statement

Parents navigating special needs care often face:

* Scattered information
* Difficulty finding specialized services
* Confusion about diagnoses and support levels

Felicity centralizes awareness and service discovery into a single, easy-to-use interface.

---

## 🧩 Features

### 🏠 Awareness Module

* Definition of special needs
* Types of disabilities (physical, developmental, behavioral, sensory)
* Severity levels (mild → severe)
* Common childhood diagnoses

### 🩺 Doctor Directory

* Pediatric specialists
* Developmental pediatricians
* Neurologists
* Clinic details with contact info

### 🧠 Therapy Centers

* Speech therapy
* Behavioral therapy
* Child development centers

### 🏫 Special Schools

* Schools supporting children with learning & developmental needs

### 🎓 Training Centers

* Skill development and structured intervention programs

### 🤝 Support Organizations

* Family support and rehabilitation centers

---

## 🛠 Tech Stack

* **Frontend:** HTML, CSS, JavaScript
* **Navigation:** Tab-based dynamic content switching
* **Design Type:** Static multi-page web application
